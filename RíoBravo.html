Río Bravo del Norte

About Río Bravo del Norte

Starting in the mountains of San Juan in the south of Colorado, Rio Bravo del Norte works as a border for Mexico and the United States.With 336,000 square miles Rio Bravo del Norte is the 20th longest in the world and fith longest in North America. Its course folws through canyons, forests, the gulf of Mexico and more.It is the only Mexican river that is not in the central part of Mexico.
