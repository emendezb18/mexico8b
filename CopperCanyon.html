About Copper Canyon

Six different canyons make up what is known as Copper Canyon. It is located in the southwestern part of Chiwawua.There are six different rivers that drain the area of copper canyon. The rivers drain into Rio Fuerte, which empties out to the Gulf of Mexico. It receives its name due to the color of its walls.The decentendents of the Aztecs tribe lives in these canyons which is why they are important to Mexico.Tourist can bike,drive, hike, and more.
