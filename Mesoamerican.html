Mesoamerican Barrier Reef

Mexico is home to the second larges barrier reef in the world. It is located along the Caribean cost line of the Yucatan peninsula and off the cost from Mexico. The reef is home to over 66 species of stony corlas and over 500 species of fish. Multiple types of marine animals can also be found here.Tourist enjoy suba diving and snorkleing, and it is one of the most popular spots to to these thing in the northern hemisphere.
